What is CoinDepo?

CoinDepo is a centralized yield platform designed for crypto holders who want supported assets to earn interest instead of remaining idle.

Supported assets & yield ranges

CoinDepo supports a mix of major cryptocurrencies and stablecoins. Yield varies by asset type, term, and product structure.

  • Major crypto assets (e.g., BTC, ETH, SOL, XRP)
  • Stablecoins (e.g., USDT, USDC) typically offer higher yield
  • Yield commonly falls in the low-to-mid double-digit APR range

Rates are not fixed and should always be verified directly inside the CoinDepo app.

How yield products are structured

Yield products are structured around time and commitment. Higher yield generally compensates for longer lockups or reduced flexibility.

  • Term-based deposits (e.g., 30, 90, 180, 365 days)
  • Simple APR vs compounded APY depending on product
  • Defined payout schedules and withdrawal rules

Security & custody model

CoinDepo presents itself as a custodial platform with enterprise-grade security controls. Asset custody, insurance representations, and audits should be reviewed directly in-app.

Always verify custody structure, insurance scope, and jurisdictional details independently.

$COINDEPO Token Advantage

Holding the native token may unlock additional benefits. Treat token exposure as a separate investment decision from yield usage.

Crypto-backed loans

Loans can provide liquidity without selling assets, but introduce liquidation risk. Conservative usage is critical.

DYOR checklist

  • Understand lockups, exits, and rollover mechanics
  • Confirm APR vs APY and compounding frequency
  • Assess platform risk versus yield compensation
  • Never allocate funds you may need liquid

Final risk notes

Yield is compensation for constraints and risk. If you cannot explain the downside, you are not ready to deploy.

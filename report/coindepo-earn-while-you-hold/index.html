What is CoinDepo?

CoinDepo is positioned as a yield platform for crypto holders who want supported assets to earn interest instead of remaining idle. Products vary by asset, rate, term length, payout schedule, and withdrawal rules.

The CoinDepo app and official documentation are always the source of truth for supported assets, rates, lockups, and eligibility.

Why “earn while you hold” matters

Most people don’t lose money in crypto because they picked the wrong asset — they lose money because they overtrade, overreact, or sit through long holding periods without a plan.

Yield strategies turn time into an input. If you already believe in an asset long term, earning yield while holding can materially change outcomes.

How CoinDepo works (simple)

  1. Deposit supported crypto or stablecoins.
  2. Select a product and term that matches your time horizon.
  3. Earn interest based on the published rate.
  4. Withdraw or roll over according to product rules.

The key variable is not the headline rate — it’s how the term, lockup, and payout mechanics align with your strategy.

APR vs APY (important)

APR is a simple annualized rate. APY typically assumes compounding. Different CoinDepo products may calculate and distribute yield differently.

Always confirm whether interest compounds automatically, pays out periodically, or accrues until maturity.

Crypto-backed loans

CoinDepo may offer crypto-backed loans, allowing users to borrow against collateral instead of selling. This introduces liquidation risk and should be approached conservatively.

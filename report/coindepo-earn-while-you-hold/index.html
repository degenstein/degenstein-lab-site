What is CoinDepo?

CoinDepo is positioned as a yield platform for holders who want supported crypto or stablecoins to earn interest instead of sitting idle.

Products vary by asset, rate, term length, payout schedule, and withdrawal rules. The in-app terms are always the source of truth.

Why earn while holding matters

Most long-term underperformance comes from impatience, not bad asset selection. Yield converts time from a liability into an input.

How CoinDepo works

  1. Deposit supported assets.
  2. Select a product and term.
  3. Earn yield per published rules.
  4. Withdraw or roll by design.

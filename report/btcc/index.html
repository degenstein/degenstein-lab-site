Overview

BTCC is one of the longest-running crypto exchanges in the industry, originally launched in 2011. Across multiple market cycles, BTCC has stayed focused on derivatives — especially futures and perpetual contracts — rather than trying to be everything for everyone.

Core Trading Focus

BTCC is built for active traders who care about execution, liquidity, and risk tools. The platform offers perpetual futures across major crypto assets and supports a range of leverage settings depending on the contract and market conditions.

Some contracts may offer leverage up to 500×. This isn’t designed for casual users — it’s for experienced traders who understand position sizing and liquidation risk.

Liquidity & Execution

Execution quality is the difference between a strategy that works on paper and one that works in real markets. BTCC emphasizes low-latency order matching and reliable performance during volatility — which is exactly when traders need the platform to hold up.

Security & Longevity

Longevity matters in crypto. BTCC has operated through multiple bull and bear markets, which puts it in a smaller category of platforms that have endured long-term.

Who BTCC Is For

  • Active and professional traders
  • Futures and derivatives users
  • Traders who value execution and liquidity
  • Users comfortable managing leverage risk

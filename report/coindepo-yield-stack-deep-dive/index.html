What is CoinDepo?

CoinDepo is a centralized crypto platform built for long-term holders who want their assets to generate yield instead of sitting idle.

Instead of focusing on trading, CoinDepo focuses on capital efficiency — allowing users to earn structured yield on crypto while still maintaining exposure to their holdings.

It combines three core pillars:

  • • Earn yield on crypto
  • • Borrow against holdings without selling
  • • Spend via upcoming crypto rewards credit card

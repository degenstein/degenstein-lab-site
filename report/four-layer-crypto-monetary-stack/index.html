BTC vs ETH vs SOL vs XRP

Crypto is converging on a four-layer monetary stack — Bitcoin as base collateral, Ethereum as settlement, Solana as execution, and XRP as liquidity clearing.

The Monetary Stack Explained

Bitcoin • Gold

Pristine collateral, censorship resistance, monetary anchor.

Ethereum • Silver

Settlement layer, programmable finance, yield-bearing collateral.

Solana • Copper

High-velocity execution, consumer-grade throughput.

XRP • Clearing

Liquidity routing between networks and institutions.

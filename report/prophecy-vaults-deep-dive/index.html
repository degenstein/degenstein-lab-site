What Is Prophecy Vaults?

Prophecy Vaults is a crypto-to-fiat platform focused on stablecoin vault locking strategies designed to generate short-term yield.

Users lock supported assets like USDT or USDC into vaults ranging from 1–7 days while earning advertised weekly bonus payouts.

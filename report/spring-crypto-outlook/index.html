Why Spring Matters

Crypto markets historically transition in spring. Liquidity conditions stabilize, volatility compresses, and capital begins positioning ahead of summer narrative expansion. This phase rewards patience, discipline, and correct exposure far more than short-term speculation.

Macro Deep Dive: The Fed Transition

A new Federal Reserve Chair expected in May represents a shift in tone before a shift in policy. Markets do not wait for rate cuts ‚Äî they respond to guidance, language, and predictability.

  • Risk assets often reprice during leadership transitions
  • Crypto historically reacts early to macro tone changes
  • Stability alone can reduce downside volatility

Regulatory Outlook: CLARITY Act Scenarios

The CLARITY Act aims to define digital asset classifications and jurisdictional boundaries. Even incremental progress reduces institutional hesitation and long-term risk premiums.

  • Clearer rules enable capital allocation
  • Compliance clarity benefits U.S.-exposed assets
  • Regulation favors infrastructure over speculation

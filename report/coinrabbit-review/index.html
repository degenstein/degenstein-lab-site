What is CoinRabbit?

CoinRabbit is a crypto-backed lending platform that allows users to unlock liquidity without selling their digital assets. Loans are issued based on collateral value rather than creditworthiness.

How CoinRabbit Works

  • Select loan currency
  • Choose crypto collateral
  • Deposit collateral
  • Receive loan
  • Repay to unlock assets

Supported Assets

CoinRabbit supports a wide range of major and alternative cryptocurrencies, making it useful for borrowers holding non-mainstream assets.

Loan Structure & LTV

Loans are overcollateralized and structured with fixed terms. If collateral value drops, borrowers may need to add funds or repay part of the loan to avoid liquidation.

Who CoinRabbit Is Best For

  • Users needing short-term liquidity
  • Crypto holders avoiding taxable sales
  • Traders bridging capital
  • Borrowers prioritizing speed and simplicity

Risk Notes

Crypto-backed loans expose users to market volatility and platform risk. Always understand liquidation thresholds before borrowing.

Final Take

CoinRabbit is a focused borrowing tool — not an investment platform. Used intentionally, it can provide liquidity while keeping crypto exposure intact.

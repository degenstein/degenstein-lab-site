Crypto-Backed Loans Explained (Quick Overview)

Crypto-backed loans allow you to borrow cash or stablecoins using cryptocurrency as collateral instead of selling it. Approval is based entirely on collateral value — not credit score, income, or location.

What Is CoinRabbit?

CoinRabbit is a crypto-backed lending platform that allows users to borrow against their digital assets instead of selling them. Loans are issued based solely on collateral value — not credit history, income, or geography.

How CoinRabbit Loans Work

  • Select the asset you want to borrow
  • Choose from a wide range of supported crypto collateral
  • Review LTV, interest rate, and liquidation thresholds
  • Deposit collateral to CoinRabbit
  • Receive funds — often within minutes
  • Repay at any time to unlock collateral

Loan Types & Repayment Flexibility

CoinRabbit supports both fixed-term and open-ended loan structures, giving borrowers flexibility depending on how predictable their capital needs are.

Fixed-Term Loans
  • Interest and duration set upfront
  • Predictable total borrowing cost
  • Best for planned, short-term needs
Open-Ended Loans
  • No fixed repayment schedule
  • Partial or full repayment at any time
  • Designed for flexible liquidity access

Crypto Loans: LTV & Liquidation Mechanics

CoinRabbit loans are overcollateralized. Your loan-to-value (LTV) ratio determines how much you can borrow relative to the value of your crypto.

  • Higher LTV provides more liquidity but less downside buffer
  • Lower LTV reduces liquidation risk during volatility
  • Collateral value is monitored in real time
  • Borrowers can add collateral or repay to restore LTV

Supported Collateral Assets

CoinRabbit supports a broad range of cryptocurrencies — including major assets and long-tail tokens — allowing borrowers to use assets they already hold.

Loan Borrowing Example (With Real Numbers)

To make CoinRabbit’s loan structure more concrete, here’s a simplified example using round numbers. This is illustrative only, but it reflects how crypto-backed borrowing works in practice.

Starting position
  • Collateral deposited: $10,000 in crypto
  • Selected LTV: 50%
  • Loan amount received: $5,000

In this scenario, the borrower retains exposure to their crypto while accessing $5,000 in liquidity without selling the underlying asset.

Market movement scenarios
  • Price stays flat: collateral remains near $10,000 — loan remains healthy
  • Price drops 20%: collateral falls to $8,000 — effective LTV rises to ~62.5%
  • Price drops further: borrower may need to add collateral or repay part of the loan

This is why conservative borrowing matters. Lower starting LTV gives you more room to absorb volatility without forced liquidation.

Why this matters
  • Borrowing too aggressively increases liquidation risk
  • Moderate LTV improves survivability during drawdowns
  • Loans should be actively monitored during volatile markets

Numbers shown are simplified for clarity. Actual thresholds and rates depend on market conditions and platform terms.

Why Borrow Instead of Selling?

  • Avoid triggering taxable sales
  • Maintain long-term market exposure
  • Access liquidity without exiting positions
  • Deploy capital while waiting for future price appreciation

CoinRabbit FAQ

What is a crypto-backed loan?

Can you get liquidated on CoinRabbit?
Yes. If collateral value drops and LTV exceeds thresholds, liquidation can occur.

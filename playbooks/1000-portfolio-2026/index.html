Goal of this playbook

Build a simple base portfolio with core exposure + a small upside sleeve — without constantly trading. This is meant for people who want to participate in 2026 catalysts while staying disciplined.

No-FOMO principle: if you’re tempted to “go all in” after a pump, your plan isn’t simple enough.

Rules (this is what makes it work)

  • No chasing pumps: if it’s up hard today, you don’t buy today.
  • Buy in steps: split entries into 2–4 buys instead of one big buy.
  • Rebalance, don’t freestyle: if one position becomes huge, trim it back to structure.
  • Don’t add risk when emotional: reduce size when stressed — not increase it.

When I add (simple triggers)

  • Red days where fear is high and nothing actually broke.
  • After consolidation (not during the vertical candle).
  • When the thesis improves (real catalysts), not when Twitter is loud.
If your reason to buy is “it’s going up,” that’s FOMO — not a plan.

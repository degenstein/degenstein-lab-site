What is CoinDepo?

CoinDepo is a crypto yield platform designed for long-term holders who want their assets to earn interest instead of sitting idle. Users deposit crypto or stablecoins into interest accounts with defined terms.

How CoinDepo Interest Accounts Work

  • Deposit supported crypto or stablecoins
  • Select a plan duration (daily, weekly, monthly, quarterly, semi-annual, annual)
  • Earn yield based on published rates (rates vary by asset and term ‚Äî can be up to ~27% depending on product, subject to change)
  • Withdraw according to plan terms

Rates, APR & APY Explained

CoinDepo advertises rates as APR or APY depending on the product. APR reflects an annualized rate, while APY can include compounding assumptions. Rates vary by asset and plan and are subject to change (some products may advertise up to ~27% depending on term and asset).

Referral Bonuses (Up to $500 USDC)

Crypto-Backed Loans

CoinDepo also offers crypto-backed loans, allowing users to borrow against deposited assets without selling. Loan terms, collateral ratios, and risks should be reviewed carefully before use. Borrowing introduces liquidation risk if collateral value drops.

Best Practices & Risk Notes

  • Rates, bonuses, and terms can change ‚Äî always verify current terms inside CoinDepo

🏛️ Real-World Assets

Real-world asset tokenization is one of the crypto sectors worth monitoring because it attempts to connect traditional financial instruments with blockchain-based issuance and settlement.

Tokenized treasuries, funds, and other asset structures should be evaluated using measurable factors such as assets under management, liquidity, issuer quality, legal structure, custody, and actual on-chain usage.

💎 Infrastructure Check

XRP is researched for payment and settlement use cases. ONDO is associated with tokenized asset infrastructure. LINK provides oracle and cross-chain infrastructure.

These are investment narratives, not guarantees. Adoption, competition, token economics, regulation, execution, and market conditions can materially change an asset thesis.

💼 Crypto Interest Accounts & Platform Risk

Interest-bearing crypto products can introduce risks that are different from simply holding an asset. Higher advertised rates do not eliminate price volatility and may add custody, counterparty, liquidity, withdrawal, operational, or platform-specific risk.

Platform Research Spotlight: CoinDepo

  • Review current advertised rates and account structures directly with the provider
  • Understand custody, counterparty, withdrawal, and platform-specific risks
  • Compare interest-account risk with self-custody and other alternatives
